Meet The Visionary Behind Influencer Badge

Founder & CEO

Jatin Kishor is a passionate entrepreneur with a vision to revolutionize the influencer marketing industry. With over 5 years of experience in digital marketing and technology, he identified the critical gap between authentic creators and premium brands seeking genuine partnerships.

His journey began while managing social media campaigns for major brands, where he witnessed firsthand the challenges both creators and brands face in finding the right partnerships. This inspired him to create Influencer Badge - a platform that brings transparency, trust, and technology to influencer marketing.

Under Jatin's leadership, Influencer Badge has grown to become a trusted platform connecting 15,000+ verified creators with premium brands, facilitating authentic collaborations that drive real results.
